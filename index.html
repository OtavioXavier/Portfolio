About me

I was born in 2005 and since then I've been passionate about computers and everything that involves them. My father introduced me to the world of hardware at a young age and I completely fell in love. My favorite pastime was disassembling, cleaning, and reassembling my computer until I discovered programming. After that, I couldn't think of anything else.

I have been studying software engineering since 2023.1 and I am part of the research center of UCSAL (Catholic University of Salvador). I work in the CIM (City Information Modelling) project, where I am the leader of the research and work. I am involved in the whole project, including the academic, back-end and front-end part.
